    Heading levels

    Below are the six standard HTML heading elements. In a real page use only one H1 and arrange the others to form a logical outline.

    H1 — Main heading

    H2 — Section heading

    H3 — Subsection heading

    H4 — Sub-subsection heading

    H5 — Smaller heading
    H6 — Least prominent heading
